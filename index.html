Welcome to the Colorverse.

The Blues have been mining color-rich asteroids in this sector for years. But on stardate zero-zero-five-two they encountered an unknown enemy for the first time... the Reds.

Controls:
Arrow Keys + Spacebar to shoot.

Instructions:
Shoot the enemy ship, or have more points when all the asteroids are gone to achieve victory. Avoid colliding with asteroids or being shot!

Tips:
You can only shoot when you're moving. The physics of the Colorverse only allow two of your bullets to be on the screen at once.

Bonus:
This game supports gamepads on Google Chrome. Just plug in and play!
